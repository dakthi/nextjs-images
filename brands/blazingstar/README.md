BlazingStar — Brand Hub

Positioning
- Professional-grade nail products focused on performance, safety, and ease of use.
- Designed for nail technicians and salons.

Origin and Identity
- Origin: Made in the UK
- Visual identity: Modern, clean, premium, trustworthy

Core Values
- Durable – Glossy – Simple – Hypoallergenic – Versatile
- Vietnamese: Bền – Bóng – Dễ Dùng – Không Dị Ứng – Phù hợp mọi phong cách

Voice and Marketing
- Tone: Professional, technical, elegant yet approachable; education-first
- Strategy: Prioritize salon owners; leverage UGC; “used by professionals” credibility
- Channels: VLDirect (B2B soon), Amazon, eBay, select distributors, trade shows
- Visual: Western style, clean studio/salon shots, neutral backdrops, focus on formula; avoid cluttered graphics

Brand Story (short)
- Built from real salon insight to deliver durability, consistency, safety (incl. TPO/HEMA-free options), and versatility across services.
- Education enables better technique → happier clients → stronger businesses.
