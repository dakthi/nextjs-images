## Marketing Alignment for Penguin Sale

### BlazingStar Strategy
- Emphasize **professional reliability** and **performance**
- Highlight **TPO/HEMA-free options** for health-conscious clients
- Position as **trusted choice for experienced technicians**
- Focus on **durability through seasons** (especially winter fast-set powders)

### BoldBerry Strategy
- Highlight **trend-forward collections** and **special effects**
- Emphasize **value for money** with bulk discounts
- Celebrate **vibrant color choices** and **playful designs**
- Market to **fast-moving salons** looking for novelty

### MBerry Strategy
- Emphasize **smooth application** and **control**
- Position as **choice for detail-oriented artists**
- Highlight **color accuracy** and **fashion-forward palettes**
- Target **upscale/boutique salon owners**
