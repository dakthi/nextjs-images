### Key Learnings Added to Instructions:

1. **Hook Strategy - Scenario/Experiential Method:**
   - For service products, spa products, seasonal offerings
   - Paint specific scenario with concrete details
   - Include business insight for salon owners
   - Longer hooks OK if they deliver context + value
   - Formula: "Mùa [X] lại tới, [người cụ thể] đang [hành động cụ thể] - [cơ hội kinh doanh]. [Business insight]"

2. **Feature Bullets:**
   - 3-4 strong bullets better than 5-7 weak ones
   - Remove redundant explanations
   - Focus on customer value perception
   - Think about customer retention, not just transaction

3. **Tone & Personality:**
   - "ạ" adds warmth in conversational tone
   - Humor in CTA OK - "làm giàu bốn mùa hihi!"
   - Business insights show expertise - "Khách đang vui rất dễ đồng ý chi thêm"
   - Like talking to a business friend, not a corporate announcement

4. **Remove Redundancy:**
   - Don't repeat hook information in paragraph
   - If hook explains context well, paragraph can be shorter or removed
   - Every section should add new information

5. **Business Psychology:**
   - Explain WHEN to upsell (festive season, group bookings)
   - Explain WHY it works (customers happy + with friends = easier to spend)
   - Position as business opportunity, not just product sale
