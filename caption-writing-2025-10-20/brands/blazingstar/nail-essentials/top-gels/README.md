# BlazingStar Top Gels

## Overview
Finishing products designed to seal, protect, and enhance gel polish applications. Each top coat offers specific protective and aesthetic benefits for different finish preferences and wear requirements.

---

## Top Gel Options

### 1. Gel Top Original
**Characteristics:**
- **Texture:** Thin, lightweight formula
- **Finish:** Natural, shiny appearance
- **Cure Time:** Fast curing under LED/UV lamp
- **Best for:** Everyday wear, natural look
- **Sizes:** 15ml, 480ml

**Application:**
- Apply thin layer over color
- Seals and protects gel polish
- Provides glossy finish
- Easy to apply, no drag

**Benefits:**
- Quick setting for efficiency
- Clean, minimalist appearance
- Versatile for all nail designs

## Application Tips

1. **Layer properly:** Apply over cured color layer
2. **Thin application:** Use thin coats for even finish
3. **Cure fully:** Allow proper curing time under lamp
4. **Avoid skin contact:** Keep away from cuticles
5. **Use with base:** Base + Color + Top = complete system

---

## Product System

**Complete Gel System:**
1. Base Gel (Original or Revive)
2. Gel Polish Color (362+ options)
3. Top Gel (Original, Durashine, or SunShield)

**Curing Requirements:**
- LED Lamp: 30-60 seconds per layer
- UV Lamp: 2-3 minutes per layer
